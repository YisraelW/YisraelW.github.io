(For Dog Owners) How to Make a Bed

  1. Chase the dog off the bed.
  2. Throw the buried bone into the dog's bed.
  3. Pick up one of the pillows to start stacking the pillow nicely.
  4. Extract the pillow from the dog's teeth.
  5. Sternly reprimand the dog.
  6. Either repair the ripped pillow or replace the pillow.
  7. Quickly arrange the pillows, sheets and blankets.
  8. Throw on a bedspread as best as you can while ignoring the whining dog at your feet.
  9. Repeat from the first step as needed.

Keep in mind the words of Groucho Marx: Outside of a dog, a book is Man's best friend. Inside of a dog, it's too dark to read.
